Welcome to Jharkhand

Jharkhand — the land of forests, waterfalls, and vibrant tribal culture. A state where tradition and nature meet modern exploration. It is known for its rich mineral resources, dense forests, and diverse wildlife. Popular tourist spots include Ranchi, Jamshedpur, and the Betla National Park which is home to tigers, elephants, and leopards. It also boasts beautiful waterfalls like Hundru and Dassam, and the serene Parasnath Hill, a significant Jain pilgrimage site. It is home to various indigenous tribes, each with their own unique customs and festivals. It is also an emerging hub for industry and education in eastern India. It is also home to the famous Baidyanath Temple in Deoghar, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. Jamshedpur, known as the "Steel City," is an industrial powerhouse and the headquarters of Tata Steel.
