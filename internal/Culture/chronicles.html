Jharkhand Chronicles (4D 3N)

Discover Jharkhand's best-kept cultural secrets on this immersive journey through government-documented villages that remain untouched by mainstream tourism. From the bustling cultural centers of Ranchi to the remote painting villages of Hazaribagh district and Saraikela, experience authentic tribal art forms that have been passed down through generations. Watch master artisans create traditional coarse paintings in their ancestral homes, explore villages so authentic they're barely findable on the internet. This isn't just sightseeing - it's cultural archaeology. Perfect for travelers seeking genuine experiences beyond the tourist trail, this tour offers unprecedented access to living heritage sites where art isn't performed for visitors, but simply exists as a way of life.



Itinerary

  • Day 1 (Ranchi)
    • Morning:

      Pickup from Ranchi Airport and transfer them hotel for freshening up followed by breakfast.

    • Afternoon:

      Provide lunch followed by visit of Audrey House and Jharkhand Kala Mandir.

    • Evening:

      Free Evening for leisure activities at a peaceful park along with providing snacks and dinner.

  • Day 2 (Hazaribagh)
    • Morning:

      Head to Hazaribagh after breakfast by bus, visit Pakri Barwadih on the way.

    • Afternoon:

      Provide lunch followed by visit of Sankriti Museum and Art Gallery.

    • Evening:

      Visit Galwan Valley and Panch Mandir, later head to hotel for dinner and rest.

  • Day 3 (Villages)
    • Morning till Late Evening:

      Freshen up and explore nearby villages for immersive experience

    • Late Evening:

      Head to Jamshedpur by bus passing through Ranchi for dinner and refreshments.

    • Night:

      Rest from long journey.

  • Day 4 (Saraikela)
    • Morning:

      1hr journey to Saraikela after breakfast by bus.

    • Late Morning to Evening:

      Indulge into Chhau Dance performances.

    • Late Evening:

      Have dinner early and drop off at Ranchi Airport.
