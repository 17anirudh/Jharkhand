Dalma Wildlife

Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary is a wildlife sanctuary located 10 km from the city of Jamshedpur in the state of Jharkhand. Inaugurated in 1975 it contains significant population of Indian Elephants.

Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls is a waterfall located 45 km from Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the state.

Patratu valley

Patratu Valley is a picturesque valley located 30 km from Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for picnics and outdoor activities.
